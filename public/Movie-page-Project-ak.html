My top 3 all-time movies.

Iron Man

Made from characters in the Marvel Comic books, this is a great science fiction / fantasy film with superb special effects, a great story, good action and humour.

Avengers

Also part of the Marvel Comic books series of films, this film brings together various super-heros like Iron Man, Captain America and the Hulk. Superb special effects, a great story, fantasic action and lots of humor.

Guardians of the Galaxy

This film and the others in the series are a must for science fiction / fantasy action fils. Also has great humour and special effects.
